About

Design is
my language

I'm Shifa Konain — a graphic designer with experience in crafting visual identities that help brands communicate with clarity and conviction.

My work lives at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics. Every mark, typeface, and color choice is deliberate — grounded in research, driven by purpose, and executed with obsessive attention to detail.

I Desire to have the privilege of partnering with startups, global agencies, cultural institutions, and ambitious independent founders across four continents.
